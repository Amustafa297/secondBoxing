About Material


GloveBox has developed a leather type glove which has a high-tech components that make your hand super safe and the gloves super durable. We use light materials such as kevlar for high strength and because it is a light and durable material. Our two major components that are used are carbon fiber and memory foam. The carbon fiber is strong and it is heat-resistant material so in that way it keeps your hands nice and cooled. The memory foam is used to create the shape of the wearer's hand for the custom fit and improved shock absorption. These materials can be combined to create a lightweight and highly protective glove, with better shock absorption and a custom fit.
